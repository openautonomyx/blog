How To Solve The Problem Of Cash

Created on 2020-07-27 07:21

Published on 2020-07-27 07:35

Almost a year back, Once I walked out of my home to buy some stuff from the near by Wellnessforver in Mumbai. I had low cash in my wallet & left the wallet at my home thinking that I would make the payment through PayTM. The shop was accepting PayTM and I had never faced any problem till then regarding payments in the shop.

But to my luck/bad luck, That day PayTM was not working in the shop due to some XYZ reason. And It was not accepting any other modes of payments.. And I had to come back home without purchasing the goods.

It made me wonder, In a world where of so many online payment options available, Can we step out of home without cash?

How many times we had to take a deroute for an ATM, how many times we have faced the problem of change (like cost of goods purchased become 23 and we had a 500 Rs note only). And its not not that our shops do not accept online payments, Many shops (At least in metro cities accept Google Pay/PayTM etc. ) but the problem still persists… Can there be a solution around this?

Today in #GetUnboxd we try to propose a solution to the problem of cash. (Disclaimer: The solution is as per my understanding of the situation, There can be a better solution to this also, And If you have a better solution please post in the comments.)

In my opinion the solution is online payment gateways like Razorpay PayU India etc. which allow you to accept payments through almost all modes of digital payments (UPI/Almost all major credit/debit cards/ Google Pay/ PayTM / Netbanking etc).

It does not take much effort to sign up for a https://razorpay.com/ or a https://payu.in/ account. And I would advise all shops/service providers (like auto rickshaw drivers/Plumbers/carpenters/Electricians) create their payment gateway accounts to be able to receive online payments through all modes of digital payments and take our country towards a cashless economy.
