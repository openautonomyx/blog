Agent systems need observability from the first prototype.

A production workflow should make every tool call, decision, retry, error, and human handoff visible. Without traces and structured logs, teams cannot explain why an autonomous system succeeded or failed.

Useful observability includes request timelines, prompt and response metadata, tool execution status, permissions, cost, latency, and evaluation outcomes.

The goal is not just monitoring. The goal is operational confidence.
